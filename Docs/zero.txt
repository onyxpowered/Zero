Zero

Zero is your local development foundation. it runs on your machine,
knows your hardware, manages your resources, and handles building and
deploying your projects in any language.

it is the beginning of every project. and the last step before it ships.


layers

  Startup    boots first. maps your filesystem, profiles your hardware,
             reads your network. writes everything it knows to information.json.
             then hands off to Build and Manage.

  Build      compiles and bundles your project in any language.
             Node.js, Python, Go, Ruby, Rust, and static sites.
             framework detection uses Zero's own engine infrastructure,
             fully integrated into Zero's build system.
             Zero controls every step.

  Manage     keeps things running. watches your resources. loads modules live
             the moment you drop them in. runs the PlugBoard — Zero's internal
             communication bus over WebSocket.

  CLI        the zero command. new, dev, build, deploy, help.
             installed globally. works from anywhere.


philosophy

  Zero has no external server dependencies.
  it talks to your OS and nothing else.
  it is local-first. always.

  it is also the deploy layer —
  from your machine to the world.
